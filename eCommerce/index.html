Online Shop

To our loyal customers — Thank you for your unwavering support over the last couple of months. With walk-up, curbside and delivery service in place, we’ve been able to stay afloat in this every changing environment.

We are excited to bring to you our online store! We will continue to remain open for walk-up service from 11 am - 7 pm Tuesday - Saturday and 12 pm - 5 pm Sunday (reminder, we are closed on Mondays). Our packaged beer, wine, crowlers, snacks, merchandise and certain homebrew supplies are available to securely and easily order online.

We strongly encourage you to order online for curbside pickup or delivery. Curbside Pickup will be ready same day as long as order is placed an hour before close. Delivery is available Tuesday - Saturday and must be submitted by 2 pm for same day delivery between the hours of 4 pm - 7 pm.

Please note that when you place an order, it will take us time to gather it together.

Please wait until you receive a confirmation email stating that your order is ready before heading to the store.

Bulk Grain: Unfortunately, we do not have the ability sell weighed goods like scoop-your-own grain through our online store. Please email info@arlbrew.com with a detailed email containing your order including quantity of each grain, whether we can combine multiple grains, and whether you would like your grains milled. We’ll be in touch via email when your order is ready.

We apologize for any inconvenience and hope that this capability will be added by our vendor soon.
